Our Story

A Clinic Built on
Trust & Care

Indira Dental Clinic was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality dental care accessible, comfortable, and affordable for every family in Kamarhati and north Kolkata.

Located at the ground floor of Sabuj Sathi Apartment on Elias Road, our clinic has grown into a trusted destination for patients of all ages — from toddlers having their first dental visit to adults seeking advanced orthodontic treatment.

We believe that great oral health is the foundation of overall wellbeing. That's why we invest in modern equipment, continual learning, and a warm, welcoming environment.
